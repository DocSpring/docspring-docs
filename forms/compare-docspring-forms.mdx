DocSpring supports three different kinds of forms that can be used to collect data and generate PDFs.

<LinkCard
  title="Web Forms"
  href="/docs/forms/web-forms/"
  description="Ideal for turning complex PDFs into a simple web-based form with inputs and checkboxes."
/>

<LinkCard
  title="Visual Forms"
  href="/docs/forms/visual-forms/"
  description="Show a visual representation of the PDF to users and allow them to fill out the fields. Similar to filling out a PDF form in Acrobat or Preview on macOS."
/>

<LinkCard
  title="Data Requests"
  href="/docs/forms/data-requests/"
  description="Generate legally binding electronic signatures with UETA/ESIGN compliance. Renders a visual form inside a secure iframe and adds compliance features and audit trails."
/>
